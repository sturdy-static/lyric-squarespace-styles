Why Lyric exists

We believe in better healthcare for everyone. Getting payments right is how we get there.
 
One in five US dollars goes to healthcare. A quarter of that is waste. Billing errors. Coding gaps. Documentation issues.
 
That waste has a human cost. Healthcare is the leading cause of bankruptcy in the United States. More than 60% those bankruptcies track back to medical debt.
 
Getting payments right across billions of claims reduces that waste. That’s exactly what Lyric does.
