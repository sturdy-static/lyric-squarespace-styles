Diagnostics Module

Our Diagnostics Module is a partnership with Concert. The science of diagnostic testing has unlocked exciting advancements in health and medicine. Unfortunately, the complexity of these tests has also led to confusion and waste for health plans, providers, and members. Together with Concert, we’re simplifying the payment accuracy of your laboratory and genetic testing claims. Unlock accurate payments, greater administrative efficiencies, low provider abrasion, and more consistent payment decisions for members and laboratories.

Lyric COB accesses deeper and distinct data insights than standard COB systems offer for complex health plan member segments through a powerful partner network that leverages advanced technology, progressive product architecture, proprietary analytics and highly specialized teams. It delivers nuanced, actionable data resulting in faster identification of overpayments or inaccurate payments.

Our Approach

Allyship underpins everything we do.

We are creating technology that is fair, transparent, and upholds privacy and safety.

  • We provide traceable rationale aligned to policy logic and coding guidance, with reviewer-visible context and auditability.
